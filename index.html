about us

You have IDEAS and we give them the WINGS

The Entrepreneurship Cell of MS Ramaiah University of Applied Sciences came into existence with the aim to promote start up culture among college students and help them find their true potential in the domain of entrepreneurship. E-cell MSRUAS is an endeavor to create opportunities for college students in realizing their dreams of starting up. We believe innovation invigorates human change and progression and the specific instrument for innovation is entrepreneurship – the act that endows resources with a new competence to create wealth. Our aim is to encourage students to instill an entrepreneurial mindset and provide all possible guidance along the way. ‎

our services

Conduct Workshops

We understand the potential of the young minds and give our support to the fullest to bring the best of out them. It is a great way to teach hands-on skills as it gives learners an opportunity to try out new methods and fail in a safe environment.

Elevate Ideas

In this competitive world, we need the future generations to be talented enough as the world craves for talent. We encourage students by educating them about diverse fields and their roles right from ideation to operation.

Conduct Seminars

Young minds that undergo the process of modification have many queries. To clear them, talks are the best. It also helps attendees do better research, to find and understand new ideas, and to disseminate their own research, etc.

Amazing Support

We believe in knowledge increases by sharing, we support the curious minds and help them nourish. We create a sustainable and easily accessible environment where the idea from the quality brains could outreach to building professionals, mentors, venture capitalists and angel investors fostering the growth of startups in India.

Operations

Operations forms the backbone of all events such as seminars, webinars and competitions in the institute. We are responsible for planning the event, estimating requisites and facilitating a conducive environment for easy functioning.

Social media and Marketing

Our team helps in developing and launching marketing campaigns for entrepreneurs. The team facilitates our outreach program to educated people even outside the boundaries of our college through online events like quiz, word banks, facts and success stories. They solicit contact with people who are interested in helping us achieve our mission goals and objectives.

Creative Designing

We have a team of talented designers who materialise ideas in an eye catching form that helps us in imparting highest quality of content with best possible impact in the most simple to understand method. They are responsible for designing of all PR materials and frequently collaborate with almost all the other teams in E-Cell.
